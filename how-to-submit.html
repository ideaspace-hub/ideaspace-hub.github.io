How to Submit Your Project for Certification

To get your certificate, you need to submit a link to your project's code on GitHub. Follow these simple steps.

Step 1: Sign Up for GitHub

If you don't have an account, create a free one at github.com.

Step 2: Create a New Repository

  1. Once logged in, click the "+" icon in the top-right and select "New repository".
  2. Give your repository a name (e.g., your project's name).
  3. Choose "Public" or "Private" visibility.
  4. Click the green "Create repository" button.

Step 3: Upload Your Project Files

  1. On your new repository's page, click the "uploading an existing file" link.
  2. Drag and drop all your project files and folders (e.g., index.html, CollabSpace Logo.png, etc.) onto the page.
  3. Scroll down and click the green "Commit changes" button to save your files.

Step 4: Get and Submit the Link

  1. After your files are saved, you will be on your main repository page.
  2. Copy the URL from your browser's address bar. It will look like https://github.com/your-username/your-project-name.
  3. Paste this link into the submission box on the Project Details page.
